"City Of Dreams"

Mumbai, often called the "city of dreams," is a vibrant metropolis located on India's western coast. It's known for its bustling atmosphere, rich culture, diverse population, and iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India and the Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus. Mumbai, city, capital of Maharashtra state, southwestern India. It is the country’s financial and commercial center and its principal port on the Arabian Sea. What is life like in Mumbai? It was built on a site of ancient settlement, and it took its name from the local goddess Mumba—a form of Parvati, the consort of Shiva, one of the principal deities of Hinduism—whose temple once stood in what is now the southeastern section of the city.
